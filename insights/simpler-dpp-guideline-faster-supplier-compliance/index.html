DPP / supplier compliance

Simpler DPP Guideline, Faster Supplier Compliance

Suppliers do not need a hundred-page DPP regulation first. They need a clear checklist or one-page SOP so they can provide trusted source data on time.

The simpler the guideline, the faster suppliers can comply.

DPP and ESG requirements become practical only when suppliers know which fields to maintain and how that source data will be used.

One Material, One Shared Identity

Repeated material identity rebuilding across suppliers, OEMs and brands is a structural communication gap.

One material should keep one shared identity across supplier, OEM, and brand.

The goal is not to force every company into one platform. The goal is to make the same material mean the same thing across different systems.

That shared identity becomes the foundation for sourcing comparison, Shared BOM, DPP readiness, and trusted source data.

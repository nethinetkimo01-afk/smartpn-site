Trusted Outcomes Require Trusted Data Sources

Decisions made from standard-zero source data cannot be fully trusted. Source data needs governance, responsibility and permission control.

Trusted outputs require trusted data at the source.

DPP, ESG, traceability, and sourcing decisions all depend on the quality of source material data.

SmartPN Atlas lets suppliers maintain source data and lets brands or OEMs reference it under permission control.

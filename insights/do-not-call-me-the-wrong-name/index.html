Do Not Call Me the Wrong Name

Repeatedly renaming the same material wastes time and money, creates inconsistent data and weakens decisions.

The same material should not become a new material every time it enters another system.

Supplier, OEM, and brand teams often describe the same material with different names and codes. Every rename creates another point of confusion.

SmartPN Atlas gives the material a shared reference so every party can keep its own system while speaking the same material language.
